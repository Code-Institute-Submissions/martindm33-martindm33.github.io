There are a few things you will need to get started running a movie night. All of the software is free, but each person watching the movie will require their own access to the streaming service you choose to use. (Netlfix, HULU etc).

Step 1 Chrome

The plugin we will use to organise the group-watch is compatible with Google's Chrome browser. This is a free browser available for download here. Once you have installed it make sure that you can succesfully login to the streaming service you are planning to use.

Step 2 - Teleparty

Head to the Teleparty website and follow their simple guide on adding Teleparty to Chrome.

Step 3 Linking Up

Once you have the plugin installed you are ready to start your movie night. Simply open your streaming service, find the film you want to watch and open it. Pause the movie and press the Teleparty plugin button to the right of the Google Chrome address bar. Tick the "Only I Have Control" box, copy the link and you're ready to start. All that remains is to share the link with your friends and each user can login and get ready for the movie.
As soon as you're ready to start simply hit play and start chatting. Talking is encouraged in this cinema!
